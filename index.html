About This Splitwise Unequal Split Calculator

This free bill splitter is optimized for itemized receipts, bulk quantities, and shared surcharges like BYO fees, card surcharges, and service charges. Use it to calculate accurate per-person totals before posting to Splitwise with “Split Unequally.” No sign-in, no tracking, and everything runs locally in your browser.

Why use this instead of direct Splitwise entry?

Splitwise does not natively handle per-item consumption or bulk packs. This tool calculates unit prices, allocations, and shared charges first, then you paste the ready totals.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, it runs entirely in the browser and stores the current session in localStorage.

What charges can I split?

Add any number of shared charges (BYO fee, service charge, card surcharge) and choose which people participate in each charge.
